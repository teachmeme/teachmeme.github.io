FAQ

What is TeachMe?

TeachMe is a online sharing knowledge platform. You can find the tutor who offer the course or skill you want to learn from TeachMe. Besides, you also can register as a tutor to earn casual income from TeachMe by teaching other people.

Is it good for me?

TeachMe can help you to find out the best tutor that near your location so that you no need to travel far for your learning course. Also, we have the rating and comment system so that user can find out the tutor that perfectly suit them easily. 

 How the payment make for the course?

TeachMe only provide online payment system to prevent fraud case happen. Student need to make the payment after they book the tutor and payment will only release to the tutor after the course finish (require both parties to click finish class on their account).

 How to register as a tutor?

For those who want to register as a tutor, they need to provide their qualification (certificate) and other relavent document such as IC, utility bill, healthy report, passport size photo and so on. The detail information will be send out by email when you register for it. Also, a short interview and training class is must before become a tutor in TeachMe.
